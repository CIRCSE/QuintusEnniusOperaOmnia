# Quintus Ennius opera omnia

The corpus *Quintus Ennius opera omnia* collects all works from the poet Quintus Ennius. It consists of the following subcorpora:
* *Annales*: fragments from the epic poem the Annals (442 fragments, 4,166 tokens)
* *Tragoediarum Fragmenta*: fragments from tragic works - cothurnatae and praetextae (204 fragments, 2,860 tokens)
* *Comoediarum Fragmenta*: fragments from comic works (4 fragments, 35 tokens)
* *E fabulis incerti nominis*: fragments from dramatic works (49 fragments, 398 tokens)
* *Satura*: fragments from satiric works (19 fragments, 677 tokens)
* *Varia*: fragments from the following works: *Scipio*, *Epigrammata*, *Sota*, *Protrepticus sive Praecepta*, *Hedupagetica*, *Epicarmus*, *Euhemerus sive Sacra Historia* (45 fragments, 1,340 tokens)

The source of the Latin text is as reproduced in the MA thesis available at [I frammenti di Ennio. Analisi computerizzata e tipologica delle varianti](https://tinyurl.com/3689h9f2).
The original text was lemmatized and PoS tagged leveraging the annotation provided in the MA thesis. The linking of the text to the Lemma Bank of the LiLa Knowledge Base was performed at CIRCSE Research Centre of the Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, Milan, Italy.

Fragments 005, 007, 011, 178, 236, 237, 257, 281, 505, 506, 709, 719, and 750 were excluded because they are historical notices, loose quotations, or passages formerly attributed to Ennius but subsequently recognized as spurious; they therefore do not represent attestations of Ennian texts.
